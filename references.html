Little Reminder:

To excel in Machine Learning and NLP, it's essential to continuously update and expand your knowledge as these fields evolve rapidly.

Data Structure and Algorithms


  • Introduction to Algorithms, fourth edition by Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, and Clifford Stein.
  • Algorithm Design by Jon Kleinberg and Eva Tardos.
  • NPTEL: Design and Analysis of Algorithms by Madhavan Mukund – An NPTEL YouTube course covering algorithm fundamentals.

Linear Algebra


  • Linear Algebra Done Right by Sheldon Axler – This best-selling textbook for a second course in linear algebra is aimed at undergraduate math majors and graduate students.
  • Lecture Notes for Linear Algebra by Gilbert Strang – A foundational textbook for linear algebra concepts.
  • NPTEL: Linear Algebra by Pranav Haridas – An NPTEL YouTube course covering linear algebra.

Probability Theory


  • A First Course in Probability by Sheldon M. Ross – A foundational textbook for probability concepts.

Theory of Computation


  • Introduction to the Theory of Computation by Michael Sipser – An upper-level undergraduate or introductory graduate text in computer science theory.

Graph Theory


  • Graph Theory with Applications to Engineering and Computer Science by Narsingh Deo – A fundamental book that covers an easy explanation of graph theory.
  • Introduction to Graph Theory by Douglas West – A thorough understanding of the structure of graphs and the techniques used to analyze problems in graph theory.


Machine Learning


  • Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow, 2nd Edition by Aurélien Géron – A practical book that uses Python for hands-on learning of ML techniques.
  • Introduction to Machine Learning, Third Edition by Ethem Alpaydın – A foundational textbook for ML concepts.
  • Stanford CS229: Machine Learning by Anand Avati – A Stanford YouTube course covering ML fundamentals and applications.
